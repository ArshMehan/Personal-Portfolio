Engineering Log

A Chronological Evolution of Mechanical Design & Robotics

System Optimization

Decode Robot V2

An advanced packaging study focused on mass centralization. By integrating all eight motors into the chassis base, this iteration achieved a significantly lower center of gravity while maintaining a compact 12"x13.5" footprint.

Modular Design

Decode Robot V1

The inaugural superstructure project prioritizing field serviceability. This design featured a modular architecture where major subsystems could be fully detached via eight primary mounting screws for rapid maintenance.

Applied Mechanics

Spur Differential PTO

A Power Take-Off system leveraging spur differentials to dynamically distribute torque. This study explored mechanical stalling and counter-rotation, incorporating custom precision dowels and modular shaft assemblies.

View Assembly
Prototyping

FF Practice Bot

A high-mobility research platform exploring the integration of a coaxial turret with a 5-DOF outtake. This project tested bare-motor belted reductions to evaluate weight distribution and mechanical response times.

Efficiency Research

Super Modular Drivetrain

An empirical study comparing high-RPM belt drive efficiency against traditional planetary gearboxes. Designed for an ultra-compact footprint, the system was tested in the 800-1000 RPM range.

View CAD
Flagship Build

Into the Deep V3

A flagship system featuring a coaxial double-arm assembly acting as a virtual four-bar linkage. This iteration prioritized technical fidelity, including full internal wire routing and COG balancing for a 6-wheel tank drive.

View CAD
Kinematics

Into the Deep V2

A kinematic study on pitching linear extensions. This project refined the 6-DOF outtake system with a focus on simplified packaging and rapid motor repair accessibility without compromising the center of gravity.

View CAD
Linkage Analysis

Into the Deep V1

Exploration of a Double Reverse Four-Bar (DR4B) mechanism. The design utilized overcenter linkages to maximize mechanical advantage and motor efficiency during high-torque hanging phases.

Experimental

Locking Mecanum

Innovative drivetrain research employing a dual-input planetary gearbox. This system allowed for a dynamic toggle between holonomic mobility and high-traction wheel locking for defensive stability.

View CAD
Advanced Research

Coaxial Swerve Drive

A deep dive into swerve kinematics, focusing on the coaxial effect. This research platform utilized master-sketching to optimize gear ratios and RPM balancing for responsive vector-based movement.

Module A Module B
Transmission Study

Differential Swerve

Analysis of an intrinsic differential transmission. This project investigated the complex gearing required to decouple wheel rotation from orientation within a compact 3D-printed superstructure.

Competitive Design

Centerstage Robot

The transition to professional fabrication methods. This robot utilized custom 3-axis CNC-routed sheet metal, a high-speed belt-drive Mecanum chassis, and integrated OpenCV vision pipelines.

Mechanical Systems

PowerPlay Robot

Iterative development of a custom steel sheet metal end effector. Prototyped via 3D printing and finalized with bent-steel construction and compliant rubber linings for secure scoring.

Foundational

Freight Frenzy Robot

The foundational project of this log. Established core competencies in full-assembly modeling, holonomic drivetrain kinematics, and the integration of vision-based navigation pipelines.
